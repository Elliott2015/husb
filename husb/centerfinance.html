Center for Financial Services

The Center for Financial Services is a vital resource for teaching, learning, and research, and it has been a showpiece for the School of Business. The activities of the Center enable faculty to integrate real-world perspectives into their courses and enhance the practical utility of education through experiential learning-by-doing for students. The Center has been instrumental in generating enthusiasm for School of Business students among recruiters. In recent years, several leading firms have been attracted toward hiring our well-prepared students, either for the first time or in larger numbers.
